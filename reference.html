About

The Gear & Triangle Honor Society is a collection of student leaders on the Stevens campus aimed to further unite our college community through principles of Honor, Fellowship, and Spirit. Since its founding in 1919, G&T has consistently made an effort to enhance all aspects of student life. Although you must complete four academic semesters before applying to Gear & Triangle, the society recognizes emerging leaders in student organizations as part of the Dean’s Activity’s List each semester. Look for us on campus and get involved!
